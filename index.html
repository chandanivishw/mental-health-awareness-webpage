Why Mental Health Matters

Tips for Better Mental Health

Small steps can make a big difference. Here are some practical ways to care for your mental well-being:

1.Talk to Someone You Trust

Opening up can relieve stress and help you feel less alone.

2.Stay Active

Exercise boosts mood, reduces anxiety, and improves sleep.

3.Limit Screen Time & Social Media

Unplug to reconnect with yourself and the real world.

4.Get Enough Rest

Prioritize quality sleep—your brain and body need it to function well.

5.Eat Balanced Meals

Nutrition impacts mood and energy—eat to fuel both body and mind.

6.Practice Mindfulness or Meditation

Just 5–10 minutes a day can help calm your mind and reduce stress.
